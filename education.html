EDUCATION

SK Seri Puleh (SKSP)
(2010 - 2016)

SKSP was my primary school from preschool until Year 6. I didn’t hold many positions during my time there, but I learned a lot from the school. I also completed my studies by sitting for the UPSR exam, although I didn’t achieve outstanding results. However, the experiences and memories I gained from SK Seri Puleh are invaluable.

SMK Tunku Mahmood Iskandar (SMKTMI)
(2017 - 2022)

SMKTMI was my secondary school, where I studied from ages 13 to 18, which it from Form 1 to Form 5. In Form 3, I sat for the PT3 examination and achieved good results. Then, in Form 5, I took the SPM examination, where I excelled and ranked among the top 20 high-achieving students. This school played a crucial role in shaping both my academic path and my life skills. The memories and experiences I gained at this school remain unforgettable.

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM)
(2022 - 2025)

After completing my SPM, I continued my studies at UiTM at the Segamat campus, where I pursued a Diploma in Information Management. This experience allowed me to expand my knowledge and skills significantly, offering numerous valuable learning opportunities at this advanced academic level.
